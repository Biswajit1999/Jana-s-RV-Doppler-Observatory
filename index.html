Stellar-Jitter Deconvolution Engine

This is the long-term differentiator: a line-by-line, multi-modal stellar activity model for separating planetary Doppler signals from photospheric noise. This is a roadmap panel, not a false claim that the engine already reaches 10 cm/s.

planned

planned
