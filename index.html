OBSERVE
High-resolution spectrographs sample stellar absorption lines thousands of times. HARPS achieves 0.5 m/s — a brisk walk. ESPRESSO reaches 0.3 m/s. Future ANDES: 2 cm/s.
〜
DETECT
The Lomb–Scargle periodogram finds periodic Doppler wobbles. Peaks above the 0.1% FAP threshold indicate a genuine planetary signal. Stellar activity creates false peaks — the core obstacle of EPRV.
⬡
CHARACTERISE
Keplerian fitting yields P, e, ω and minimum mass M sin i. The CCF bisector slope (BIS) and FWHM diagnose whether a signal is astrophysical or instrumental. This observatory reproduces that pipeline.
